PRODUCT NAME: EVA - AI Personal Assistant (AI On Call)

PRODUCT TYPE: AI-powered IVR / Personal Call Assistant (VAS - Value Added Service)

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:
EVA is an AI Personal Assistant that answers your phone calls when you are busy, unavailable, or don't want to pick up. EVA talks like a real human -- she greets callers, understands why they are calling, takes messages, schedules meetings by syncing with your Google/Outlook/Apple calendar, and sends you an instant summary of every call via WhatsApp, SMS, or the telco's preferred messaging channel. If the call is urgent, EVA sends a flash message notification immediately. EVA filters out spam calls, robo calls, sales calls, and promotional calls so you never have to deal with them again.

HOW IT WORKS (4 Simple Steps):
1. SUBSCRIBE: Activate EVA instantly via USSD, SMS, or app. Press 1 to subscribe.
2. SET UP: Name your EVA and sync your calendar for seamless scheduling.
3. STAY CONNECTED: EVA answers calls you don't pick up or disconnect. She talks to callers naturally like a human assistant.
4. GET UPDATES: Receive real-time call summaries via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. Never miss important information.
